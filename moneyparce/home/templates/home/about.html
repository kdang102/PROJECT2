About

MoneyParce is a web application with a goal to help users manage their everyday spending. Using the app, users would be able to: Track their income, Track their spending, Set spending limits for budgeting, and Get tips and tricks on how to reduce spending

With MoneyParce you will see just how useful, practical, and relatable personal finance is.



Tips

- Check your budget before making any purchases!-
- Use the graphs to help visualize your spending!
